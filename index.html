Fire Hydrant Compliance Program

Ensuring your fire hydrants are operational is a critical responsibility. Our all-inclusive program helps you meet all legal and insurance obligations with zero hassle.

Meeting Regulatory Standards

Whether for a municipality or a commercial property, all fire hydrants must be properly maintained and fully operational. We help you navigate the requirements under provincial law to ensure full compliance.

  • The Ontario Fire Code stipulates that hydrants must be checked and maintained annually.
  • Insurance providers require proof of maintenance to validate policies, especially in the event of a fire.
  • Only licensed water operators are legally authorized to operate hydrants connected to a potable water system.

Our All-Inclusive Maintenance Agreement

What's Included
