Fire Hydrant Compliance Program

Our all-inclusive program is designed to keep you in compliance with all regulations. It features proactive maintenance and includes all necessary repairs to ensure your fire hydrants remain fully operable and ready for service.

What's the deal with fire hydrants?

They are a critical part of fire protection infrastructure. As the owner, you are responsible for ensuring they are properly maintained and fully operational in accordance with provincial law.

Key Compliance Points:

  • The Ontario Fire Code mandates that hydrants be inspected and maintained annually.
  • We adhere to industry best practices, including post-use inspections to guarantee readiness.
  • Because hydrants are connected to the drinking water system, only licensed water operators are legally authorized to operate them.
  • Detailed records of all maintenance and inspections must be kept and available upon request.
  • Hydrants must be kept clear and accessible to fire department vehicles at all times, including being free of snow and ice.

Doesn't the water department handle it?

Not always. While municipal checks occur, they may not meet the full scope of regulations for specific properties. We provide the detailed report you need for your records, ensuring the ultimate responsibility is covered.

How do I find a qualified contractor?

You already have. Watersome's licensed operators have over 20 years of experience and are ready to partner with you on a comprehensive annual maintenance program that covers everything required by law.

Water Mains & Valves

The backbone of any water distribution network, water mains and valves require expert care to ensure reliable service and prevent costly failures. We provide a full suite of services for municipal and commercial systems.

  • Emergency Repair: 24/7 response for watermain breaks to minimize service disruption and property damage.
  • New Installation: Professional installation of new watermains and system components according to all engineering standards.
  • Valve Maintenance: Proactive programs to exercise and service critical valves, including the use of our hydraulic valve turner to safely operate seized valves.
  • Valve Box Cleaning: We utilize our mini-vac for precise and non-destructive cleaning of valve boxes to ensure easy access and operation.
  • System Flushing & Swabbing: Unidirectional flushing (UDF) and swabbing to maintain water quality and improve flow.

Sewer & Storm Systems

Properly functioning sewer and storm systems are essential for public health and environmental protection. We offer comprehensive services to manage and maintain this critical infrastructure.

  • Sewer Main Services: Cleaning, inspection, and repair of sanitary and combined sewer mains to ensure optimal flow and prevent backups.
  • Sewer Lateral Repair: Trenchless and conventional repairs for the service lines connecting properties to the main sewer system.
  • Storm System Maintenance: Proactive cleaning and maintenance of storm drains, catch basins, and mains to prevent flooding.
  • New Installations: Expert installation of new sewer and storm infrastructure for developing areas.
